About The Robo-Tech Forum

Welcome to the The Robo-Tech Forum, where technology meets innovation! Our club is dedicated to exploring the exciting world of robotics, engineering, and automation.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a platform for students to learn, experiment, and build robots that can solve real-world problems. We believe in fostering creativity and teamwork in the field of robotics.

What We Do?

We work on various projects, from simple robots to complex autonomous systems. Our club members have access to state-of-the-art tools and resources to bring their ideas to life.. Our club offers comprehensive programs designed to provide practical experience with modern robotics technologies:

  • Robotics Workshops: Hands-on sessions covering programming, electronics, mechanical design, and system integration
  • Competition Teams: Participation in DD Robocon, IRoC-U, and regional engineering & Robotics competitions
  • Personal Projects: Collaborative work on autonomous systems, machine learning applications, and innovative prototypes
  • Industry Partnerships: Company Sponsorships and internship opportunities
  • Technical Skills Development: CAD, 3D printing, microcontroller programming, and project management

Our History

Founded with a vision to democratize robotics education, our club has grown from a small group of enthusiasts into a thriving community of innovators.

2013
Founded in 2013 by some bunch of passionate tech enthusiast Engineers to share and explore the vast knowledge of Robotics. The main target of the founders was to target the DD Robocon Competition.
2017
First time the club got The Mathworks Modelling Award. This was the biggest and the greatest achievement for the whole Club which still is celebrated.
2018
This was the second time The Robo-Tech Forum was recognized and Considered worthy of the Mathworks Modelling Award, 2018. This makes our achievement to be twice in a row.
2023
The Robo-Tech Forum was recognized by E-Yantra Lab of IIT, Bombay which lead us to gaining the access of Cutting Edge E-Yantra Lab Robotics Components, deepening our knowledge in the world of Robotics.
2024
The Club now comes under the Department of Electronics and Telecommunicaton, Government College of Engineering, Amravati.
2025
We were able to secure All India Rank 12 in DD Robocon which marked our growth and development in the field of Robotics.
